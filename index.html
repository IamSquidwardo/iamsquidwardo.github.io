OUT

The idea for the movie "Out" had been in the works since februuary 2019, with writing starting from the next month, there was a long script writing and casting process until the filming started the following april. On set everyone involved had a very enjoyable time and looked forward to the movies release.

The movie was released in august 2021 to glowing reviews from fans and critics alike, with some decribing it as "a heartfelt look at the lives of normal people", it has got 4.6 stars in reviews from the public.
